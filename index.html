Postdoc · Tsinghua University, Beijing, China

Email: duanch@tsinghua.edu.cn

I am Chunhui Duan (段春晖), currently a Postdoc Research Fellow in School of Software, Tsinghua University. Previously, I received the B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the School of Software at Tsinghua University, in 2013 and 2018 respectively, supervised by Prof. Yunhao Liu.

My research interests include RFID, indoor localization, wireless network, and mobile sensing.
